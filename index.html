Let's keep our community safe

How to prevent from Corona19

  • Wash your hands often. Use soap and hand sanitizer with water or alcohol.
  • Keep a safe distance from people who cough or sneeze.
  • Wear a mask when it is impossible to keep physical distance.
  • Don't touch your eyes, nose, or mouth.
  • Use toilet paper or bend your elbow to cover your nose and mouth when coughing or sneezing.
  • Take a rest at home if you are not feeling well.
  • If you have fever, cough, or difficulty breathing, you should consult a doctor.

Coronavirus Symptoms

Common symptoms include:

  • Fever
  • A dry cough
  • Feeling tired
In rare cases, the following symptoms may occur:
  • Body ache
  • Sore throat
  • Diarrhea
  • Conjunctivitis
  • Headache
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Skin rash, finger or toe discoloration
Severe symptoms include:
  • Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • Chest pain or pressure
  • Language or motor impairment
